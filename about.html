About Us

My journey started with a small YouTube channel where i share heartfelt recipes made by my mother. Each dish isn’t just food it’s a feeling of home, and a reminder of love. Over time, the love and encouragement from my viewers has grown so much that I felt it was time to take the next step. That simple wish inspired me to open this restaurant a place where I could bring the same homemade flavors from my mom’s kitchen to your table. Every recipe here is rooted in tradition, made with love, and served with the warmth only a mother’s hand can offer.


"Come and experience the true taste of home just like mom used to make."
